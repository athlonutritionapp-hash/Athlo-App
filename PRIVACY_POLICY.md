# Privacy Policy for Nutrition App

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Nutrition app ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### User Profile Data
- Sport preference
- Age (optional)
- Weight (optional)
- Height (optional)
- Fitness goals
- Dietary preferences

### Usage Data
- Chat conversation history
- Favorite/bookmarked studies
- Generated nutrition overviews
- Study search history

## How We Store Your Data

**All data is stored locally on your device** using iOS UserDefaults and local file storage. We do not:
- Send your data to external servers (except API requests to Groq for AI responses)
- Sync your data to cloud services
- Share your data with third parties
- Track your usage across sessions

## Third-Party Services

### Groq API
- **Purpose:** AI-powered chat responses
- **Data Sent:** Your chat messages are sent to Groq's API to generate responses
- **Privacy:** Groq's privacy policy applies to data sent to their service
- **Retention:** We do not store your messages on external servers

### Public Research APIs
- **PubMed:** Used to fetch scientific studies (no personal data sent)
- **Semantic Scholar:** Used to fetch research papers (no personal data sent)

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted except for:
  - API requests to Groq (chat messages only)
  - Public API requests for research studies (no personal data)

## Your Rights

- **Access:** All your data is stored locally - you can view it in the app
- **Deletion:** You can clear chat history and reset your profile at any time
- **Export:** Currently not available, but all data is accessible within the app

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at athlonutritionapp@gmail.com

## Data Retention

- Chat history: Stored locally until you delete it
- User profile: Stored locally until you reset it
- Cached responses: Automatically expire after 24 hours
- Studies: Stored locally and shared across app sessions

## No Tracking

We do not use tracking technologies, analytics services, or advertising networks. The app does not collect usage analytics or personal identifiers.
